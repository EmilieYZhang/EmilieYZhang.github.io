About Me

Personal Interests

From a young age, I have been interested in writing stories and crafting the creative arts. From drawing on traditional paper, to writing short stories, I have tried it all.
I have a great interest in travel and photography as well. Last summer, I went to Disney in Orlando, Florida. It was one of the best experiences in my life.
I should mention that I am a very curious person, and one who would not back down from a challenge.
Whether it be solving a puzzle, or learn Franz Liszt's "La Campanella" on the piano, I always try my best.
Finally, helping others is a quality which I value a lot. I am interested in making a difference in the world and help others feel happier.
